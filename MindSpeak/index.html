Mental Health Awareness

MindSpeak is a non-governmental, non-profit awareness platform dedicated to promoting mental well-being among students and adults. While our services are primarily delivered online, our initiative is based in Malaysia and works closely with educational institutions and community partners to support mental health education.

About This Website

This Mental Health Awareness website is an informational platform developed to educate and support people. It provides reliable information in a simple and non-judgmental manner.

The website encourages people to prioritize their mental well-being and seek help when necessary.

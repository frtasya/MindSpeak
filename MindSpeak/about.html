About Mental Health

What is Mental Health?

Mental health refers to a personâ€™s emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It influences how individuals think, feel, act, handle stress, relate to others, and make decisions in daily life.

Maintaining good mental health helps individuals function effectively and cope with the normal stresses of life.

Importance of Mental Well-Being

Good mental well-being helps individuals remain productive, build meaningful relationships, and maintain a positive outlook on life.

Prioritizing mental well-being improves resilience, enhances self-confidence, and supports overall quality of life.

Common Misconceptions

Many people believe that mental health issues are rare or a sign of personal weakness. In reality, mental health challenges are common and can affect anyone.

Seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness, and early support can lead to better outcomes.
